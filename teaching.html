Teaching and Instructional Experience

Luiss Guido Carli University

  • Project-Based Organizing — Jul-2025
    • Taught a summer course for executives at Leonardo S.p.A., Managing stakeholders in a project.
    • Overall rating score 81.3 on a scale of 100. Received a standing ovation at the conclusion of the course.
  • Research Project Design* — Spring 2021; Fall Semesters 2023–2025
    • Led three class sessions per semester on the fundamentals of qualitative research methods, including data collection, data analysis, coding, and interactive exercises.
    • Received an instructor rating of 83.6 (in 2023), 92.2 (in 2024), and 88.5 (in 2025) on a scale of 100.

    • Received an instructor rating of 100 (in 2023), 91.7 (in 2024), and 100 (in 2025) on a scale of 100.
  • International Operations and Supply Chain* — Fall Semester 2023
    • Led three sessions on managing social issues in the textile industry. Invited two guest speakers (Asim Jofa, CEO of Asim Jofa, and Charlotte Piller, CEO of Lotta Ludwigson) and moderated discussions on supply chains in both circular and fast-fashion business models.
  • Corporate Strategy* — Spring Semesters 2021, 2022
    • Led two sessions: one on core theoretical frameworks in strategy and another on the fundamentals of strategy simulation games.
  • Management of Innovation* — Fall Semester 2021
    • Led a session on how organizations innovate in collaboration with stakeholders, featuring the teaching case of Asim Jofa.
